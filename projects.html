Our Projects

We focus on high-impact, scalable initiatives that meet real needs in our community.

Rakt Jagriti Abhiyan

Free blood group check & smart card distribution

Why this matters

Early knowledge of blood groups saves lives during emergencies. We conduct free on-site camps, collect youth data, run awareness talks, and issue smart cards that include key tips for safe donation.

How it works

  • Organize camps in schools, colleges, and communities
  • Free blood group testing by our volunteers & lab partners
  • Issue smart membership cards with blood group & donation guidance
  • Stage-2 ceremony with renowned personalities to distribute cards

Insurance Support for Families

50 families to start • goal 5000

What we provide

We guide underprivileged families to access suitable health insurance schemes, helping them navigate documentation and enrollment to reduce out-of-pocket costs.

Scale-up plan

We plan to expand from 50 to 5000 families through partnerships with insurers, hospitals, and CSR programs.

Monthly Medicines for the Elderly

50+ beneficiaries and growing

Problem

Many elderly or chronically ill patients skip medicines due to cost. This leads to preventable complications.

Our response

We identify vulnerable beneficiaries and provide monthly medicines via local pharmacies and donors.

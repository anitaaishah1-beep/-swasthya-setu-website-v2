What we do

  • Free blood group check & Smart Card distribution (Rakt Jagriti Abhiyan)

  • Monthly medicines for 50+ elderly / chronic patients
  • Insurance support for 50 families (scaling to 5000)
  • Coming soon: Rakt Mitra – a donor-matching app
